Welcome to the Dwarf Library!

Here you will be able to find about books regarding the Dwarf fantasy race. From lore to short stories, this is a centralized database of information about the race, indiscriminate from setting.

These are the documents that you will find in this website:

  • About different Dwarfs and how they are portrayed in their specific universes and medias.
  • All lore to date of Dwarfs of specific universes:
    • Comprehensive character list of notable characters which are Dwarfs.
    • Story of their civilization, their customs and how they perceive others.
    • Comprehensive list of cities and what they are known for.
